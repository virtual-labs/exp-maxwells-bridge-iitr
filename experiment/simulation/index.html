STEP 1 : Make all the Correct Connections.

1.    M1 of MCB - P1 of POWER

2.    M2 of MCB - P2 of POWER

3.    OS1 of Oscillator - OS3 of Oscillator

4.    OS2 of Oscillator - OS4 of Oscillator

5.    S1 of Speaker - B of Circuit Diagram

6.    S2 of Speaker - D of Circuit Diagram

7.    COM - C of Circuit Diagram

8.    L1 - D of Circuit Diagram

STEP 2 : Click on "CHECK" Button.    

STEP 3 : Click on the MCB to Turn ON the circuit.

STEP 4 : Set Oscillator to maximum from dropdown.

STEP 5 : Select the values from various dropdowns to get minimum frequency sound from speaker.

STEP 6 : Click "ADD" button to add the values to the Observation Table.

STEP 7 : If combination is wrong then select another combination.

STEP 8 : Add different values to the Table by selecting different values from dropdown.

STEP 9 : Click on "PRINT" button to print the page.
